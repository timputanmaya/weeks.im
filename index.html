WEEKLY ACTIVITY CALENDER

Kids Activities

Week 1

Title: Painting Description: Coney ideas,express emotion,use senses. Instructions: Give each child an image to see and follow it. Notes: Remember to bring a bag,water,paint brushes and A4 size papers.

Completed

Week 2

Title: Drawing Description: A way of making a picture and exploring it. Instructions: Give each child an image to see,understand and bring their talent out by drawing it. Notes: Remember to bring a bag,pencils,caryons,pastels and A4 size papers.

Completed

Week 3

Title: Swimming Description: The propulsion of body through water by combined joints. Instructions:Enter water with instructor holding their arms to stay afloat. Notes: Bring water tube,swimming suit,cap,goggles.

Completed

Week 4

Title:Music Description:An art of sound in time that expresses ideas and emotions in significant forms through melody. Instructions:Start with basic Sa Re Ga Ma Pa. Notes: Bring a book, pen,earphnones.

Completed

Week 5

Title:Chess Description:A two player strategy mind game where the aim is to move different types of playing piece,on a square board. Instructions:Each player takes a move at a time white&black in a unique way. Notes: Bring a book, pen,earphnones.

Completed

Week 6

Title:Hockey Description:A game played on foot with hockey stick and with a small ball. Instructions:Players can drag the ball from the opponent's side into their field. Notes: Bring a hockey stick,ball,wearing helmet and shoes.

Completed

Week 7

Title:Cricket Description:A game played between 2 teams each of 11 per team with a bat and ball on the field. Instructions:One team will bat first and the second team goes next. Notes: Bring a bat,ball,safe guards,wearing helmet and shoes.

Completed

Week 8

Title:Dance Description:A form of creative expression through physical movement that includes rhythmicmovements that accompany music. Instructions:It is poignant and hopeful. Notes: Bring a water bottle,wear shoes.

Completed

Week 9

Title:Boxing Description:A sport that involves strategically punching an opponent while defending yourself. Instructions:The punch should be below the belt or back of the opponent's head not on the neck. Notes: Wear gloves,helmet,tooth clips.

Completed

Week 10

Title:Taekwando Description:An art of self defence originated in Korea. Instructions:Keep your attention,bow,ready and start.. Notes: Wear mentioned white dress with a belt.

Completed
